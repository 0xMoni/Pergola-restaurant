Our Story

A journey of flavors and traditions

The Pergola Story

Founded in 2010, Pergola began with a simple vision: to create an extraordinary dining experience that celebrates the rich tapestry of Indian cuisine while embracing modern culinary innovations. Our journey started with family recipes passed down through generations, each dish telling its own unique story.

Our Philosophy

At Pergola, we believe that great food is more than just taste - it's an experience that brings people together. Every dish we serve is crafted with passion, using the finest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create memorable dining moments.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of chefs and staff work tirelessly to ensure that every visit to Pergola is special. With decades of combined experience in Indian cuisine, our chefs bring expertise and creativity to every dish they prepare.

Our Values

Quality

We source only the finest ingredients and maintain the highest standards in food preparation.

Tradition

We honor traditional recipes while adding our own contemporary twist.

Innovation

We continuously explore new flavors and techniques to enhance our menu.

Hospitality

We treat every guest like family, ensuring a warm and welcoming experience.
